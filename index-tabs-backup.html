Our Mission

"We make everything fresh: the meats, the vegetables, the sauces. Nothing in cans. We make everything every morning."

We're a family-owned restaurant proudly serving the Kathleen and Warner Robins community. Greek food is simple and elegant with flavors subtle to robust, textures smooth to crunchy. Dining at Olympia Gyros is an adventurous journey into the cradle of civilization.

Every dish is prepared fresh daily using authentic recipes and quality ingredients. From our classic gyros to our signature pasta dishes, we put care into everything we serve.
